If you have a question or need assistance, please reach out to us through the appropriate channels:

### Bug Reports
If you encounter an issue, please report it to our [Issue Tracker](https://github.com/faubulous/mentor-vscode/issues) so we can investigate and fix it. Your feedback helps us understand what works well and what we can improve.

### Questions & Answers
Feel free to ask in the [Mentor Discussions](https://github.com/faubulous/mentor-vscode/discussions) forum.

### Reviews and Feedback
Share your experience with Mentor by leaving a review on the [Visual Studio Marketplace](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=faubulous.mentor).
