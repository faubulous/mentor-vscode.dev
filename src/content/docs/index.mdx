## Features
Mentor offers a wide range of features to help you efficiently develop your projects:

### Workspace

<LinkCard
  title="Workspace Index"
  href="/features/workspace-index/"
  description="Find usages and quickly jump to subject definitions across files in your workspace."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Workspace Tree"
  href="/features/workspace-tree/"
  description="Navigate and inspect all ontology and SPARQL files in the project to quickly find and identify problems across the workspace."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Definitions Tree"
  href="/features/definitions-tree/"
  description="Explore and manage the definitions of classes, properties, shapes, and concepts the current document with support for structural OWL reasoning."
/>

### Editor

<LinkCard
  title="Syntax Highlighting & Validation"
  href="/features/syntax-highlighting/"
  description="Highlighting and linting of N-Tripeles, N-Quads, Turtle, Trig, RDF/XML and SPARQL syntax."/>

<LinkCard
  title="Prefix Management"
  href="/features/prefix-management/"
  description="Auto-implement and manage namespace prefixes in your project."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Refactoring"
  href="/features/refactoring/"
  description="Rename prefixes and resource labels, sort and remove unused prefix definitions, and more."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="SPARQL Query"
  href="/features/sparql-query/"
  description="Write and execute SPARQL queries with auto-completion, syntax highlighting, and validation."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Notebooks"
  href="/features/notebooks/"
  description="Create and run interactive notebooks combining SPARQL queries, RDF content and Markdown documentation."
/>

### Collaboration

<LinkCard
  title="Live Collaboration"
  href="/features/live-collaboration/"
  description="Run Mentor in the browser on vscode.dev and edit GitHub repositories online."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Git Integration"
  href="/features/git-integration/"
  description="Seamlessly integrate with Git to manage version control and collaborate with your team."
/>
