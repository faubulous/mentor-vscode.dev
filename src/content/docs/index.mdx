## Features
Mentor offers a wide range of features to help you efficiently develop and manage your knowledge management and application projects:

### Workspace

<LinkCard
  title="Workspace Index"
  href="/guide/workspace-index/"
  description="Find usages and quickly jump to subject definitions across files in your workspace."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Workspace Tree"
  href="/guide/workspace-tree/"
  description="Navigate and inspect all ontology and SPARQL files in the project to quickly find and identify problems across the workspace."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Definitions Tree"
  href="/guide/definitions-tree/"
  description="Explore and manage the definitions of classes, properties, shapes, and concepts the current document with support for structural OWL reasoning."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Settings"
  href="/guide/settings/"
  description="Share Mentor settings across your team by committing workspace settings to your repository."
/>

### Editor

<LinkCard
  title="Syntax Highlighting & Validation"
  href="/guide/syntax-highlighting/"
  description="Highlighting and linting of N-Tripeles, N-Quads, Turtle, Trig, RDF/XML and SPARQL syntax."/>

<LinkCard
  title="Prefix Management"
  href="/guide/prefix-management/"
  description="Auto-implement and manage namespace prefixes in your project."
/>

<LinkCard
  title="Refactoring"
  href="/guide/refactoring/"
  description="Rename prefixes and resource labels, sort and remove unused prefix definitions, and more."
/>

### Development

<LinkCard
